ABOUT US

For all your finance needs...now and in the future!

Australian Credit Corporation has been in the business of assisting people with their lending needs since 1998. In this time we have helped clients with different lending needs, from small personal loans through to multi million dollar funding facilities.

The team have many years experience in both the bank and non bank sector which has helped us understand what our clients are looking for. Our aim is to offer cost effective lending solutions that will be suitable now and in the future.

In doing this, we believe you, our client, will save money in the long and short term. Our clients appreciate the personal service offered by not having to deal with automated phone systems and that any enquiry is personally and competently handled by someone who they have dealt with before that understand their requirements.

We look forward to assisting you with your lending needs now and in the future.
